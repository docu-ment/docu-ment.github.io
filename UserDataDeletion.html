Delete Your Account

To delete your account and associated data from our servers, follow these steps:

  1. Open the App.
  2. Go to the Stats window, at the bottom right corner.
  3. Select the gear icon to open the options.
  4. Scroll down and select "Delete Account".
  5. Type in "delete" to confirm deletion and accept to permanently delete your account.
